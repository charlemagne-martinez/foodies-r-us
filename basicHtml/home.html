Couch Potato Saga Presents

CouchPotato Saga presents an up-and-coming platform for food enthusiasts called Foodies R Us! This platform currently manages a database of over 20,000 restaurant locations that users have explored throughout the U.S. With over 1 million active users and growing, Foodies R Us is experiencing rapid growth. Such growth presents the challenge of handling vast amounts of data. As such, Foodies R Us recognizes the need for a robust data-driven application to support its expanding user base. This application will record user-generated data, including restaurant names and record of reviews. With this system in place, Foodies R Us can provide a positive user experience, facilitate quick searches for preferred dining spots, and offer valuable insights into popular or trending locations.

Entities

  • Locations
    Locations represent specific geographic areas where restaurants can be found. They encapsulate information such as city, state, and country. In scenarios where multiple instances of the same restaurant exist in the same city, these restaurants will share the same location entry in the database.
  • Users
    Users represent individuals who interact with our website. They are required to provide personal information such as first name, last name, email, username, and password during registration. Users utilize the website to explore restaurants, leave reviews, and engage with other features.
  • Restaurants
    Restaurants are establishments where users dine. Each restaurant entry includes details such as its name, cuisine type, and possibly a reference to its parent chain if applicable. Users can explore restaurant listings, view details, and leave reviews for their dining experiences.
  • Restaurant Chains
    Restaurant Chains represent overarching businesses that own or operate multiple restaurants. This entity includes details about the chain's name and possibly additional descriptive information. Chains may have multiple restaurant locations under their umbrella.
  • Reviews
    Reviews are feedback provided by users regarding their dining experiences at restaurants. Each review includes details such as the user who left the review, the restaurant being reviewed, a rating, comments, and the date of the review. Reviews help other users make informed decisions about where to dine.
  • Cuisine Types
    Cuisine Types represent different types or styles of cuisine offered by restaurants. This entity includes a list of various cuisine types such as Italian, Chinese, Mexican, etc. Each restaurant typically offers one cuisine type, and this information helps users filter and search for restaurants based on their culinary preferences.
