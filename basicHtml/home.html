Introducing Foodies R Us Admin Portal, providing an exclusive administrative view of our extensive restaurant database. With over 20,000 restaurant locations meticulously cataloged across the U.S., our platform serves as the ultimate resource for food enthusiasts. Designed to cater specifically to administrators, Foodies R Us Admin Portal addresses the challenge of efficiently managing vast amounts of data. Through this specialized portal, administrators can access and manage user-generated data, including restaurant names and review records. By harnessing the power of data, Foodies R Us Admin Portal empowers administrators to optimize user experience, streamline restaurant searches, and gain valuable insights into popular and trending dining spots.

Entities

  • Locations
    Locations represent specific geographic areas where restaurants can be found. They encapsulate information such as city, possibly state, and country.
  • Users
    Users represent individuals who interact with our website. They are required to provide personal information such as first name, last name, email, username, and password during registration. Users utilize the website to explore restaurants, leave reviews, and engage with other features.
  • Restaurants
    Restaurants are establishments where users dine. Specifically, restaurants are single, concrete establishments. Each restaurant entry includes details such as its name, cuisine type, and possibly a reference to its parent chain if applicable. Users can explore restaurant listings, view details, and leave reviews for their dining experiences.
  • Restaurant Chains
    Restaurant Chains represent overarching businesses that own or operate multiple restaurants. This entity includes details about the chain's name and possibly additional descriptive information. Chains may have multiple restaurant locations under their umbrella.
  • Reviews
    Reviews are feedback provided by users regarding their dining experiences at restaurants. Each review includes details such as the user who left the review, the restaurant being reviewed, a rating, comments, and the date of the review. Reviews help other users make informed decisions about where to dine.
  • Cuisine Types
    Cuisine Types represent different types or styles of cuisine offered by restaurants. This entity includes a list of various cuisine types such as Italian, Chinese, Mexican, etc. Each restaurant typically offers one cuisine type, and this information helps users filter and search for restaurants based on their culinary preferences.
